TOP 5 CITIES OF NEPAL

4. BHARATPUR

Bharatpur is a city located in the Chitwan District of Nepal, situated approximately 146 kilometers west of Kathmandu. The city is known for its natural beauty, including the Chitwan National Park, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, which is home to a variety of wildlife, including tigers, elephants, and rhinoceroses. Visitors to Bharatpur can explore the park on foot, by jeep or on elephant back, to get an up-close view of the natural wildlife. The city is also known for its traditional Tharu culture, with several Tharu villages located nearby. Visitors can experience Tharu dance performances, traditional handicrafts, and sample local cuisine. With its stunning natural beauty and rich cultural heritage, Bharatpur is a must-visit destination for anyone traveling to Nepal.

Places not to miss while visiting Bharatpur:

1. Chitwan National Park: A UNESCO World Heritage Site, known for its rich biodiversity and home to endangered species like the one-horned rhinoceros and the Bengal tiger.

2. Narayani River: A popular spot for rafting and other water activities, offering beautiful views of the surrounding hills and forests.

3. Kasrra Durbar: A historical palace built by the Rana rulers, known for its unique architecture and intricate wood carvings.

4. Bishazari Tal: A beautiful lake with over 20,000 lotus flowers, offering a peaceful atmosphere and a perfect spot for bird watching.

5. Tharu Cultural Museum: A museum that showcases the culture and lifestyle of the indigenous Tharu people, with traditional clothing, utensils, and artifacts.

Visiting these places will make your trip to Bharatpur a memorable one, and will give you a glimpse into the natural beauty and rich culture of Nepal.
