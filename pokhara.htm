TOP 5 CITIES OF NEPAL

2. POKHARA

Pokhara is a city located in the western region of Nepal, situated approximately 200 kilometers west of Kathmandu. It is known for its stunning natural beauty, including the picturesque Phewa Lake and the panoramic views of the Himalayan mountain range, including the famous Annapurna and Dhaulagiri peaks. Pokhara is also a popular destination for adventure sports such as paragliding, trekking, and white-water rafting. The city is home to several temples and Buddhist monasteries, including the World Peace Pagoda, a stunning white stupa situated on a hilltop overlooking the city. Visitors to Pokhara can also explore the lively Lakeside area, which is filled with restaurants, bars, and shops, as well as nearby villages and waterfalls. With its stunning natural scenery and rich cultural heritage, Pokhara is a must-visit destination for anyone traveling to Nepal.

Places not to miss while visiting Pokhara:

1. Phewa Lake: A stunning lake located in the heart of Pokhara, with crystal clear water and surrounded by mountains.

2. Sarangkot: A popular viewpoint located at an altitude of 1,592 meters, offering panoramic views of the Annapurna Range and Pokhara Valley.

3. World Peace Pagoda: A Buddhist stupa located atop a hill, offering stunning views of the surrounding mountains and Pokhara Valley.

4. Davis Falls: A beautiful waterfall located near the Gupteshwor Mahadev Cave, with an interesting story behind it.

5. Gupteshwor Mahadev Cave: A sacred cave that houses a shrine of Lord Shiva, with impressive stalactites and stalagmites.
6. Pokhara Museum: A museum that showcases the history, culture, and traditions of the Pokhara region.

7. International Mountain Museum: A museum that offers a comprehensive insight into the history and culture of the Himalayas.
